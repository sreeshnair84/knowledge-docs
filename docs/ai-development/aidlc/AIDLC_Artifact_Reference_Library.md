# AIDLC Artifact Reference Library — Templates & Examples

Live templates for all 8 AIDLC phases and the full TOGAF 10 ADM artifact set, with field definitions and example content for every artifact.

> **Audience:** Enterprise Architects, AI Delivery Leads, Program Managers, Governance Teams
> **Coverage:** All 8 AIDLC Phases · EA Artifacts · TOGAF 10 ADM Phases · Phase Gates · Template Field Definitions
> **As of:** 2026

This library covers 40+ artifact templates — from the AI Use Case Charter that opens AIDLC Phase 1 through the EA Cross-Cutting artifacts an EA team maintains indefinitely — each shown with full field definitions and worked example content for a hypothetical "Credit Risk AI Scoring Model" use case at a financial services enterprise. It is split into four parts by topic boundary: two covering the AIDLC delivery-lifecycle phases, and two covering the TOGAF 10 ADM enterprise-architecture governance layer that wraps around an AIDLC delivery.

## Series parts

| Part | Title | What's unique to this part |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | [AIDLC Lifecycle Artifacts: Discovery Through Model Design](./AIDLC_Artifact_Reference_Library_Part1_Discovery_to_Model_Design.md) | Phases 1–4 (Discovery & Ideation, Feasibility & Risk Assessment, Data Strategy & Governance, Model Design & Architecture) — the "how to read this document" legend, and every artifact from the AI Use Case Charter through the draft Model Card |
| 2 | [AIDLC Lifecycle Artifacts: Development Through Retirement](./AIDLC_Artifact_Reference_Library_Part2_Development_to_Retirement.md) | Phases 5–8 (Development & Training, Evaluation & Red-Teaming, Deployment & MLOps, Monitor/Audit & Retire) plus the cross-lifecycle Enterprise Architecture artifacts (AI System Inventory, Agent Action Boundary Register, Data Lineage Map) |
| 3 | [TOGAF ADM Artifact Templates: Foundation Through Technology](./AIDLC_Artifact_Reference_Library_Part3_TOGAF_Foundation_to_Technology.md) | The TOGAF 10 ADM legend and AI-First extension conventions, then the Preliminary Phase, Architecture Vision (A), Business Architecture (B), Data Architecture (C1), Application Architecture (C2), and Technology Architecture (D) artifact templates |
| 4 | [TOGAF ADM Artifact Templates: Migration Through EA Cross-Cutting](./AIDLC_Artifact_Reference_Library_Part4_TOGAF_Migration_to_EA_CrossCutting.md) | Opportunities & Migration (E/F), Implementation Governance (G), Architecture Change Management (H), EA Cross-Cutting artifacts, and the two cross-reference summary tables covering the entire library |

Both the AIDLC-phase templates (Parts 1–2) and the TOGAF ADM-phase templates (Parts 3–4) trace to the same illustrative example — a Credit Risk AI Scoring Model — so field values and artifact IDs stay consistent whichever part you're reading.

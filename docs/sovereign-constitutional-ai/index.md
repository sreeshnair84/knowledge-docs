**Audience:** Chief AI Officers, AI Governance Leads, Principal AI Architects, AI Risk Officers, AI Security Architects, Enterprise Architects, Board Advisors

## Mission

Create the definitive body of knowledge for designing, governing, securing, auditing, and operating Sovereign Constitutional AI systems. The scope goes beyond traditional Responsible AI (RAI) and AI governance to encompass the full spectrum of sovereign, constitutional, trustworthy, and aligned AI — enabling organizations, governments, banks, healthcare systems, telecom operators, defense organizations, and critical infrastructure providers to establish AI systems that are **aligned, explainable, auditable, sovereign, constitutional, compliant, safe, resilient, and governable**.

# Enterprise Strategy & Business Architecture Handbook

> **Audience:** CIOs, CTOs, Chief Strategy Officers, Enterprise Architects, Business Architects, AI Architects, Program Managers, Product Leaders, and Management Consultants.
>
> **Purpose:** A comprehensive, practitioner-grade reference covering every dimension of enterprise strategy, business architecture, and operating model design — from first principles through 2026 AI-era practices.

---

## How to Use This Handbook

This handbook is organized into five volumes, each self-contained but heavily cross-referenced. Read end-to-end for a complete education; use individual volumes as reference during active engagements.

| Volume | Covers | Best For |
|--------|--------|----------|
| [Vol 1 — Corporate Strategy](./vol1-corporate-strategy) | Strategy hierarchy, types of strategy, strategic planning | CSO, CEO, Senior EA |
| [Vol 2 — Business Architecture & Operating Models](./vol2-business-architecture-operating-model) | Capability maps, value streams, TOM design | Business Architect, EA |
| [Vol 3 — Portfolio Management & Governance](./vol3-portfolio-governance) | PMO, investment planning, enterprise governance | PMO Lead, Program Manager |
| [Vol 4 — Consulting Frameworks & Industry Strategy](./vol4-consulting-frameworks-industry) | McKinsey/BCG/Bain tools, industry playbooks | Management Consultant, EA |
| [Vol 5 — AI Strategy, Transformation & Glossary](./vol5-ai-strategy-transformation-glossary) | AI strategy, transformation roadmaps, A-Z glossary | AI Architect, CTO |

## Concept Hierarchy at a Glance

```
ENTERPRISE CONTEXT
│
├── STRATEGY LAYER
│   ├── Vision / Mission / Purpose
│   ├── Strategic Intent → Themes → Priorities → Objectives
│   ├── Initiatives → Programs → Projects
│   └── Strategic Roadmap
│
├── ARCHITECTURE LAYER
│   ├── Business Architecture (Capabilities, Value Streams, Processes)
│   ├── Application Architecture (Systems, Integrations, APIs)
│   ├── Data Architecture (Domains, Products, Lineage)
│   └── Technology Architecture (Platforms, Infrastructure, Cloud)
│
├── OPERATING MODEL LAYER
│   ├── Organization Design (Structure, Roles, Spans)
│   ├── Governance Model (Decision Rights, Committees)
│   ├── Delivery Model (Agile, Product, Platform)
│   └── Support Model (DevOps, SRE, FinOps, MLOps)
│
├── PORTFOLIO LAYER
│   ├── Strategic Portfolio → Programs → Projects
│   ├── Product Portfolio → Epics → Features → Stories
│   └── Investment Planning → Benefits Realization
│
└── AI TRANSFORMATION LAYER
    ├── AI Strategy → AI Capabilities → AI Operating Model
    ├── AI Platform → AgentOps → LLMOps → PromptOps
    └── Governance → Responsible AI → AI Risk
```

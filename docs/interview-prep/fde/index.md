# Forward Deployed Engineer — Interview & Study Hub

The **Forward Deployed Engineer (FDE)** is one of the fastest-growing roles in enterprise AI: a senior technical practitioner who embeds directly inside a customer organisation to ship production AI systems, then hands ownership cleanly back to the client team.

FDE job postings grew **729% year-on-year (April 2025 → April 2026)**. Anthropic, OpenAI, Palantir, Scale AI, AWS, Databricks, and EY are all actively building FDE functions. Total compensation at top-tier AI companies ranges **$350K – $750K**.

---

## What Makes FDE Different

| Dimension | Forward Deployed Engineer | Enterprise Architect | Solutions Architect |
|-----------|--------------------------|---------------------|---------------------|
| Primary output | Shipped production system | Architecture blueprint | Presales design |
| Customer depth | Embedded (weeks–months) | Advisory | Proposal-time |
| Code ownership | Yes — post-launch | No | No |
| Revenue motion | Post-sale delivery | Strategic advisory | Pre-sale |
| Success metric | Measurable business KPI | Adoption + governance | Deal close |
| Typical cycle | 10–14 weeks per engagement | 6–18 months per initiative | Days to weeks |

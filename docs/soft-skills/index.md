# Non-Technical Skills

Voice mastery, storytelling, and communication training for technical leaders.

This section contains **four skill areas**: two thinking-skill guides (problem-solving and creative thinking) and two communication-training programs (a 28-day starter track and a full 12-week deep track for senior technical leaders). Read the overview below before choosing where to start.

---

## Thinking Skills

| Document | What it covers |
| --- | --- |
| [Problem-Solving Skills](Problem_Solving_Skills) | Structured problem decomposition (MECE / issue trees), root-cause analysis (5 Whys + Fishbone / Ishikawa), hypothesis-driven investigation, and a decision framework for moving from diagnosis to action. Includes a self-assessment to identify your weakest problem-solving phase. |
| [Creative Thinking Skills](Creative_Thinking_Skills) | Five frameworks for divergent thinking: Lateral Thinking (de Bono), Six Thinking Hats, Reframing techniques (including the Pre-Mortem), SCAMPER, and "How Might We" possibility thinking. Includes a quick-reference framework selector by situation. |

**Relationship:** Problem-solving is **convergent** — it narrows hypotheses and diagnoses root causes. Creative thinking is **divergent** — it expands options and reframes problems before you converge. Use them in sequence: reframe first, decompose second.

## The Two Programs

### Track 1 — The 28-Day Starter (general-purpose)

Three documents work together as one program:

| Document | What it covers |
| --- | --- |
| [Voice Training 30-Day Plan](Voice_Training_30Day_Plan) | Day-by-day practice schedule across 4 weeks — breathing, articulation, delivery, live reps. Uses Yoodli, Toastmasters, and TED-Ed as free tools. |
| [Mental Models for Voice Training](Mental_Models_for_Voice_Training) | 8 thinking frameworks (First Principles, Inversion, Feedback Loops, and 5 more) mapped directly to each week of the 28-day plan. Helps you practice smarter, not just harder. |
| [Storytelling Exercise Workbook](Storytelling_Exercise_Workbook) | Fillable workbook teaching the STAR method and 3-Act structure. Includes story worksheets, an emotion-mapping exercise, a self-scoring checklist, and a Personal Story Library template. |

**Relationship:** Start with the 30-Day Plan as your schedule. Use the Mental Models guide as a daily thinking companion alongside it. Add the Storytelling Workbook in Week 4 when the plan introduces storytelling practice.

### Track 2 — The 12-Week CTO Voice Mastery Program (senior technical leaders)

| Document | What it covers |
| --- | --- |
| [CTO Voice Mastery Program](CTO_Voice_Mastery_Program) | An 84-day, day-by-day coaching transcript synthesizing Vinh Giang (vocal mastery, 4 archetypes, storytelling), Julian Treasure (HAIL framework, vocal toolbox), and Chris Voss (tactical empathy, mirroring, calibrated questions). Targeted at a 20-year senior architect moving into CTO-level communication. |

**Relationship:** This is a standalone deep track. It covers all the vocal foundations in Track 1 and extends them significantly — adding the Hormone Storytelling Framework, a 12-week Story Bank build, influence tools, executive presentation structure, and a final performance capstone on Day 84.

## How the Two Programs Relate

The **storytelling frameworks are complementary but use different terminology** — intentionally. The 30-Day Plan and Workbook teach the **STAR method and 3-Act structure** (widely used, work-presentation focused). The CTO Voice Mastery Program teaches **Vinh Giang's Hormone Framework** (Dopamine / Oxytocin / Endorphin / Cortisol) and his **7 Storytelling Secrets** (CTO-context focused).

Both introduce a personal story library concept:
- The Workbook calls it a **Personal Story Library** — a short, fillable template for 5 general work stories.
- The CTO program builds a **Story Bank** — a deeper archive of 10–20 career stories developed over 12 weeks using Vinh Giang's 5-field template.

These are compatible, not competing. If you work through both, your Personal Story Library from the Workbook can feed directly into your CTO program Story Bank.
